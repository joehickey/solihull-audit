Training effects
Wilcoxon tests indicate significant increases on all three SKCQ scales after training.

PROQOL effects 
Wilcoxon tests indicate signifcant increases in professional quality of life after training.

Associations
Spearman correlations indicate significant association between staff's rating of confidence to use Solihull concepts after a whole-day training, and self-rated compassion and burnout at six-month follow-up.
